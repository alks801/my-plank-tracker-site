Quick troubleshooting

  • Restart the app.
  • Restart your iPhone.
  • Make sure you’re on the latest app version from the App Store.
  • If stats look wrong, check if you used Reset All Data in Settings.

FAQ

  • Is an account required? No. The app works without sign-in.
  • Where is my data stored? On your device (local storage).
  • Can I disable sounds/haptics/countdown? Yes, in Settings.
  • How is streak counted? Based on your daily completed sessions.
