This Privacy Policy explains how Plank Tracker handles your data. The app is designed to be privacy-first and does not require any user account.

Data Collection

Plank Tracker does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All workout data (timers, streaks, statistics, and settings) is stored locally on your device.

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email, location, or device identifiers.

Analytics & Tracking

Plank Tracker does not use third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising frameworks. Your activity is not tracked across apps or websites.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using secure system storage. If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

The app does not share data with any third-party services.

Children’s Privacy

Plank Tracker does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected, no child data is stored or processed.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be published on this page.
